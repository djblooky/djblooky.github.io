Brain Agents

Level Designer, Unity Developer, & Production Assistant


Brain Agents is a game for change being developed for iOS and Android mobile devices in partnership with the non-profit agency Stryv 365 to help youths develop trauma resilience.


I have contributed to preproduction documentation, UI wireframing, as well as the level design of 8 unique stages and their implementation in Unity. I am also responsible for scripting several core gameplay interactions and systems in C# and assisting to organize the project workflow in GitHub Project Boards.
