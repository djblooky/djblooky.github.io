Steven Universe: Unleash the Light

Assistant Producer


Steven Universe: Unleash the Light was developed by Grumpyface Studios and released as an Apple Arcade exclusive that is available to play on iPhone, iPad, and Apple TV.


As the Production Assistant I worked out of Cartoon Network Digital's office in Atlanta, GA in the Summer of 2019 working closely with the Lead Producer, Eric Cook. I assisted with documenting creative feedback, localization, and multi-platform QA testing.
